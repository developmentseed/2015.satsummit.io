--- layout: session title: Collaboration between Satellite and Development Sectors time: 15:30 - 16:15 type: Panelists moderator: Alexander Howard people: - Saara Romu - Albert Anoubon-Momo - Jeremy Hale --- The global development community and satellite companies stand to benefit immensely from shared understanding of needs and capabilities. Development organizations will need to retool and rethink their practices to best take advantage. Satellite companies are interested in supporting the work of development organizations, but they lack detailed strategy and insight into the needs of development and humanitarian communities. This panel will discuss how altering perspectives and methods can lead to better collaboration between practitioners and providers.
