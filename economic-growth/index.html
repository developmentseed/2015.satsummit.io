--- layout: session title: Investing in Economic Growth and Social Empowerment through Earth Observation time: 13:45 - 14:30 type: Panelists moderator: Paul Goodman people: - Thomas Gillespie - Larisa Serbina - Rose Jackson - Alex MacDonald --- The United States Government has made huge investments in earth observation. Programs like Landsat, MODIS and GPS all of which have huge economic and social impacts beyond government use cases. This panel will look at how the government has invested resources and funds to promote the US Aerospace industry and to advance social and economic goals.
