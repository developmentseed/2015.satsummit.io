--- layout: session title: The State of Earth Observation for Global Development time: 10:00 - 10:45 type: Panelists moderator: Marc Prioleau people: - Bruno Sanchez-Andrade Nuno - Kass Green - Hunter Goldman - Gustavo Faleiros --- The satellite industry is rapidly advancing -- new sensor technology, improved access to imagery, and increased computing power for analysis. Satellite imagery and big data tools provide powerful insights into issues central to global development: climate change, population growth, and natural resource availability. This panel will discuss the current state of play in applying satellite data to development challenges.
